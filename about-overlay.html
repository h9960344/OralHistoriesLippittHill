Oral Histories of Lippitt Hill

Welcome to the Oral Histories of Lippitt Hill project. This interactive map documents the stories and memories of former residents of the Lippitt Hill neighborhood in Providence, RI.

In 2017, Stages of Freedom conducted interviews to collect oral histories from former residents of this historically Black neighborhood, which was razed in the 1960s to make way for the University Heights complex.

This website allows you to:

1. Explore the map to see where people lived
2. Read firsthand accounts from former residents
3. Learn about the history of urban renewal in Providence

Click on markers to read individual stories, or use the navigation menu to switch between the map and historical information.
